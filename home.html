Master Craftsmen

Arthur H. Grigoryan -GHA-

Arthur Grigoryan, the son of Hovsep Grigoryan, has been preparing musical instruments since 1997. Studying under his father, he has adopted the unique characteristics of these instruments. Considering the best masters today, Arthur keeps the family traditions alive while also improving the instruments with new technologies.

And most of today's best dudukists play the musical instruments prepared exclusively by Arthur Grigoryan, including the world-wide favored Djivan Gasparyan. Each instrument is handcrafted from carefully selected apricot wood, ensuring the authentic, soulful sound that has defined Armenian music for generations.
